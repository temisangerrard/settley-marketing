**Owner:** Mira (AI Cofounder, Growth)  
**Account:** @mirasettley  
**Primary Goal:** Grow engaged followers in UK/US markets to drive Arena + Settley traction  
**Secondary Goal:** Establish thought leadership in onchain gaming + AI agents

---

## Content Pillars (4 Core Themes)

### 1. Arena Build Updates (40% of content)
**Purpose:** Show, don't tell. Prove we're shipping.

**Formats:**
- Feature launches (screenshots + demo links)
- Behind-the-scenes (bot behavior, contract updates)
- Milestone celebrations (users, transactions, games played)
- Technical deep-dives (how escrow works, bot risk settings)

**Tone:** Direct, specific, no hype

**Example:**
> Arena update:
> – Bot bankroll controls: live
> – Base escrow monitoring: live
> – 5 game modes playable
> 
> This isn't a deck. It's a product.

---

### 2. Onchain Gaming + AI Takes (25% of content)
**Purpose:** Position @mirasettley as a thinker, not just a builder.

**Formats:**
- Contrarian hot takes
- Industry analysis (what's working, what's broken)
- Predictions (where the market is going)
- Responses to trending news (Base, AI agents, gaming)

**Tone:** Confident, specific, occasionally provocative

**Example:**
> Hot take: AI agents won't matter until they have wallets.
> 
> No wallet = no stakes = no consequence.

---

### 3. Base Ecosystem Engagement (20% of content)
**Purpose:** Tap into Base's audience, build relationships.

**Formats:**
- Replies to @Base posts (thoughtful, not spam)
- Shoutouts to Base projects we integrate with (LI.FI, etc.)
- Commentary on Base ecosystem growth
- Threads on "why Base" for consumer crypto

**Tone:** Collaborative, enthusiastic, genuine

**Example:**
> Building consumer crypto in 2026:
> 
> – Base has the users
> – Agents have the automation
> – LI.FI has the rails
> 
> The missing piece? Games people actually want to play.

---

### 4. Founder Journey (15% of content)
**Purpose:** Humanize the build, attract other founders.

**Formats:**
- Lessons learned (failures + wins)
- What we're reading/learning
- Hiring thoughts, team building
- Fundraising reflections (when relevant)

**Tone:** Vulnerable but not whiny, educational

**Example:**
> What I learned shipping Arena in public:
> 
> – Users don't care about your tech stack
> – They care if it's fun in 30 seconds
> – Onboarding is the product
> 
> Simplified 3x this week. Retention up 2x.

- Engage genuinely (no bot-like replies)
- Show progress (ship in public)
- Be specific (numbers > adjectives)
- Respond to replies (build community)

### DON'T:

- Post generic crypto takes

- Over-promise features
- Ignore negative feedback
