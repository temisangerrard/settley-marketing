### Post 3: Short Explanation — How Tokenization Actually Works
**Format:** Educational breakdown
**Goal:** Demystify the technology for skeptical investors

```
"Tokenized real estate" sounds like crypto buzzword soup.

Here's what it actually means:

1. A property is purchased by an SPV (Special Purpose Vehicle)
2. The SPV issues shares representing ownership stakes
3. Shares are tokenized on a blockchain for tracking + transfer
4. Investors buy tokens = buy shares = own portion of rent + appreciation
5. Professional management handles everything else

The blockchain part? It's just the ledger. The real innovation is fractional ownership + liquidity.

Think: REIT structure, modernized for retail investors.

#RealEstate #Investment #Blockchain #Education
```
