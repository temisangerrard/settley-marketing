## Content Rules
1. Post without overthinking
2. Use market data we already have
3. Join existing conversations
4. Share genuine insights
5. Focus on education over promotion

## Quick Response Templates

### Property News
"Interesting development in [LOCATION]! This is exactly why we're seeing [YIELD]% returns in our target areas. Here's what this means for investors: [INSIGHTS]"

### Market Questions
"Great question! We've analyzed [X] properties in [CITY] and found that [DATA POINT]. This is why we're making it possible to invest from just £[AMOUNT]."

### Tech Discussions
"This is exactly why we built our platform with [FEATURE]. Property investment shouldn't require [PROBLEM]. We're solving this by [SOLUTION]."

## Key Metrics to Reference
- Manchester yields: 6-8%
- London appreciation: 2.1% YoY
- Min investment: £500
- Target markets: 2 cities
- Student population: 85,000+

## Emergency Topics to Avoid
- Specific return promises
- Financial advice
- Regulatory claims
- Competitor criticism
- Unverified data
