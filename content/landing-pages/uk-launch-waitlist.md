## Problem Section

**Headline:** Property Investment Is Broken for Regular Investors

**Body:**
92% of UK adults are locked out of property investment.

Traditional buy-to-let requires:
- £50k+ deposit (25% of average UK home)
- Mortgage approval (income thresholds, credit checks)
- Landlord responsibilities (tenants, repairs, regulations)
- High transaction costs (stamp duty, legal fees, agent fees)

**The result?** Property wealth is concentrated among the already-wealthy. Regular savers get 0.5% in easy access accounts while property owners build generational wealth.

## FAQ Section

### Is Settley FCA-regulated?
**Yes.** Settley operates under FCA cryptoasset regulations (CP25/28, CP25/40, CP25/41). We structure tokens as restricted mass market investments (RMMI) with appropriateness assessments for all investors.

**What this means for you:** Investor protections, clear risk disclosures, FCA oversight.

### What are the risks?
**Property investment carries risks:**
- Property values can fall (capital at risk)
- Tenants can default (void periods)
- Platform risk (Settley operational failure)
- Liquidity risk (secondary market may be thin)

**We mitigate through:** Diversification (10+ properties), professional management, FCA compliance, transparent reporting.

**Full risk warning:** You may lose some or all of your invested capital. Only invest what you can afford to lose. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

### How do I earn returns?
**Two return streams:**
1. **Rental income** — Monthly distributions from tenant rent (target: 4-8% annual yield)
2. **Capital appreciation** — Property value increases over time (target: 2-5% annual)

**Total expected return:** 6-12% annual (based on 2024 UK property performance)

### Can I sell my tokens?
**Yes.** Settley operates a secondary market for token holders. Sell fractions anytime to other investors. Fees: 2-3% (vs. 5-8% for traditional property sales).

**Liquidity note:** Secondary market liquidity depends on buyer demand. In early stages, sales may take days/weeks. As the marketplace grows, liquidity improves.

### What's the minimum investment?
**£100 per property.** You can invest £100 in one property or spread £100 across 10 properties (£10 each).

**Recommended:** Start with £500+ to diversify across 5+ properties.

### Where are properties located?
**Phase 1 (Q3 2026):** Manchester (high yields, strong tech scene, affordable entry)
