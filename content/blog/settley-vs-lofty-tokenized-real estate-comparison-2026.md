## The Real Problem for UK Investors

Here's what most comparison articles won't tell you: **Lofty.ai wasn't built for UK investors.**

When you invest in Lofty.ai as a UK resident, you face:

### 1. Currency Risk
Every USD/GBP exchange rate movement affects your returns. A 10% GBP strengthening against USD wipes out your rental yield for the year.

### 2. Tax Complexity
You need to:
- File W-8BEN forms with Lofty.ai
- Report US income to HMRC
- Navigate US-UK tax treaty implications
- Potentially deal with US estate tax thresholds ($60k for non-residents)

### 3. Market Misalignment
US rental markets behave differently from UK markets:
- Different tenant protection laws
- Different property appreciation patterns
- Different economic drivers
- Different interest rate environments

### 4. No Local Exposure
You're betting on US property markets while living in the UK. You understand UK property dynamics better than Tulsa, Oklahoma (where Lofty has properties).
