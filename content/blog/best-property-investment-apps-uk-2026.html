How We Tested and Ranked These Apps

We evaluated each property investment app against six criteria:

  1. FCA Regulation — Is the platform authorised and regulated? What type of permission do they hold?
  2. Minimum Investment — How much do you need to start? Can you diversify with small amounts?
  3. Historical Returns — What have investors actually earned? Are returns target or achieved?
  4. Liquidity Terms — How long is your money locked? Is there a secondary market?
  5. Mobile App Quality — Is there a native iOS/Android app? How's the user experience?
  6. Transparency — Do they publish default rates, deal performance, and clear risk warnings?

We also cross-referenced Trustpilot reviews, FCA register entries, and industry reports to verify claims. Where platforms have experienced problems (defaults, wind-downs, payment delays), we've flagged them prominently.
