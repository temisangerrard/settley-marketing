## FAQ

### Is Settley FCA-regulated?

Settley operates under FCA tokenisation guidance (CP25/28) as a private placement. We are not a regulated fund manager — tokens represent equity in a UK SPV (Limited Company) that holds the property. This structure exempts us from public prospectus requirements while maintaining investor protections (whitelist, appropriateness assessments, 10% cap).

### Can I invest if I'm not in the UK?

Pilot phase: UK residents only (simplifies legal/tax structure). Post-pilot: We'll explore EEA and international investors, but UK compliance is our priority.

### What happens if the property loses value?

Property values can go down. Token value reflects underlying property value. You can lose money. Only invest what you can afford to hold long-term (3-5 years minimum).

### How do I exit my investment?

Phase 1 (pilot): Hold-to-rent (dividend income). Phase 2 (Q4 2026): Whitelist secondary market (sell to other verified investors). Phase 3: Property sale (majority vote triggers sale + capital distribution).

### Is this crypto?

Tokens are blockchain-based digital securities representing SPV equity. They use Ethereum/Polygon (not speculative cryptocurrencies). Value derives from property cash flow + appreciation, not token speculation.
