# Headline Formulas That Convert

**Source:** Proven formulas from direct response copywriting  
**Use:** When writing headlines for landing pages, emails, ads, social posts

---

## 🎯 Core Formulas

### 1. **Achieve X Without Y**
> "Understand how users are really experiencing your site **without** drowning in numbers"

**Template:** `[Desirable outcome] without [pain point]`

**Examples:**
- "Own property from £100 without becoming a landlord"
- "Earn passive income without managing tenants"
- "Invest in real estate without £50k deposits"

---

### 2. **The [Opposite] Way to [Outcome]**
> "The **easiest** way to turn your passion into income"

**Template:** `The [adjective] way to [achieve outcome]`

**Examples:**
- "The simplest way to diversify into property"
- "The fastest way to earn rental income"
- "The safest way to invest in real estate"

---

### 3. **Never [Unpleasant Event] Again**
> "**Never miss a sales opportunity again**"

**Template:** `Never [pain point] again`

**Examples:**
- "Never miss a property investment opportunity again"
- "Never worry about tenant management again"
- "Never deal with estate agent fees again"

---

### 4. **[Feature/Product] for [Audience] to [Outcome]**
> "An online whiteboard **for teams to** ideate and brainstorm together"

**Template:** `[Product] for [audience] to [outcome]`

**Examples:**
- "Property investment for beginners to build wealth"
- "Tokenized real estate for UK investors to earn passive income"
- "Fractional ownership for first-time investors to diversify"

---

### 5. **You Don't Have to [Skill/Resources] to [Outcome]**
> "With Ahrefs, **you don't have to be an SEO pro** to rank higher and get more traffic"

**Template:** `You don't have to [barrier] to [outcome]`

**Examples:**
- "You don't have to be rich to invest in property"
- "You don't have to be a landlord to earn rental income"
- "You don't have to have £50k to start investing in real estate"

---

### 6. **[Achieve Outcome] by [How]**
> "Generate more leads **by seeing** which companies visit your site"

**Template:** `[Achieve outcome] by [mechanism]`

**Examples:**
- "Earn passive income by owning property fractions"
- "Diversify your portfolio by investing in real estate tokens"
- "Build wealth by owning fractions of UK properties"

---

### 7. **[Key Benefit]**
> "Sound clear in online meetings"

**Template:** `[Single strongest benefit]`

**Examples:**
- "Own UK property from £100"
- "Earn monthly rental income"
- "Sell property tokens anytime"

---

### 8. **[Question Highlighting Pain Point]?**
> "Hate returning stuff to Amazon?"

**Template:** `[Question about pain point]?`

**Examples:**
- "Tired of estate agent fees?"
- "Frustrated with property investment minimums?"
- "Want rental income without landlord headaches?"

---

### 9. **Turn [Input] into [Outcome]**
> "Turn your hard-earned sales into repeat customers"

**Template:** `Turn [input] into [outcome]`

**Examples:**
- "Turn £100 into monthly rental income"
- "Turn spare cash into property ownership"
- "Turn property investment into passive income"

---

### 10. **[Number] [People/Things] Use [Product] to [Outcome]**
> "10,000+ investors use Settley to earn passive income from property"

**Template:** `[Social proof] use [product] to [outcome]`

**Examples:**
- "150+ early users earn rental income with Settley"
- "67% of users convert to paid members"
- "7 properties tokenized, £2M+ invested"

---

## 📊 Formula Selection Guide

| Goal | Best Formula | Example |
|------|--------------|---------|
| **Address objection** | Achieve X Without Y | "Invest without £50k" |
| **Simplicity** | The [Opposite] Way | "The easiest way to..." |
| **Eliminate pain** | Never X Again | "Never miss opportunities" |
| **Clarity** | [Product] for [Audience] | "Property investment for beginners" |
| **Lower barrier** | You Don't Have to X | "You don't have to be rich" |
| **Mechanism** | Achieve X by Y | "Earn by owning fractions" |
| **Direct benefit** | [Key Benefit] | "Own property from £100" |
| **Empathy** | [Question]? | "Tired of estate fees?" |
| **Transformation** | Turn X into Y | "Turn £100 into income" |
| **Social proof** | [Number] use X | "150+ users earn income" |

## 🧪 Testing Framework

**Test each headline on:**

1. **Clarity** — Can a 10-year-old understand it?
2. **Specificity** — Does it include numbers or concrete details?
3. **Relevance** — Does it address the reader's pain/desire?
4. **Urgency** — Does it create reason to act now?
5. **Credibility** — Is it believable (not hype)?

**Score:** 1-5 on each. Total ≥20 = strong headline.

## 🚫 Common Mistakes

### ❌ Too Vague
> "The Future of Real Estate Investment"

**Fix:** Add specifics
> "Own UK Property from £100. Earn Monthly Rental Income."

---

### ❌ Too Long
> "Welcome to Our Revolutionary New Platform That Will Change How You Think About Property Investment Forever"

**Fix:** Cut 70%
> "Property Investment from £100"

---

### ❌ Feature-Focused
> "Blockchain-Based Tokenization Platform with ERC-20 Compliance"

**Fix:** Convert to benefit
> "Own Property Fractions. Sell Anytime. Earn Monthly."

---

### ❌ No Proof
> "Industry-Leading Returns with Minimal Risk"

**Fix:** Add data
> "6-12% Annual Returns (Based on 2024 UK Property Performance)"

---

### ❌ Multiple Messages
> "Invest in Property, Earn Passive Income, Diversify Your Portfolio, and Build Wealth Today"

**Fix:** ONE message
> "Earn Monthly Rental Income from £100"
