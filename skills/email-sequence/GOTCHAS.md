# Email Sequence Gotchas

**Last Updated:** 2026-03-17  
**Source:** Common failures from email marketing campaigns

---

## 🚫 Most Common Mistakes

### 1. **Too Many CTAs Per Email**
**Wrong:**
> "Check out our blog, follow us on Twitter, join our waitlist, download the whitepaper, and book a demo"

**Right:**
> "Join the waitlist → Get early access" (ONE primary CTA)

**Why:** Decision paralysis kills conversions. One email = one job.

---

### 2. **Asking Before Giving Value**
**Wrong:**
> Email 1: "Buy our product"  
> Email 2: "Seriously, buy it"  
> Email 3: "Last chance to buy"

**Right:**
> Email 1: "Here's a free resource to help you"  
> Email 2: "Quick win you can achieve in 10 minutes"  
> Email 3: "Story: How we solved this problem"  
> Email 4: "Ready to go deeper? Here's our product"

**Why:** Earn the right to sell. Value first, ask second.

---

### 3. **Vague Subject Lines**
**Wrong:**
> "Update from our team" or "Important information"

**Right:**
> "Get your first property investment in 10 minutes" or "The mistake 90% of new investors make"

**Why:** Vague = ignored. Specific = opened.

---

### 4. **Sending Too Frequently**
**Wrong:**
> Daily emails for 2 weeks straight

**Right:**
> Welcome: Immediate  
> Email 2: Day 2  
> Email 3: Day 4  
> Email 4: Day 7  
> Email 5: Day 12  
> Email 6: Day 20

**Why:** Email fatigue = unsubscribes. Space for digestion.

---

### 5. **No Segmentation**
**Wrong:**
> Same sequence for: new signup, paying customer, churned user

**Right:**
> New signup → Welcome sequence  
> Paying customer → Onboarding/activation sequence  
> Churned user → Re-engagement sequence

**Why:** Relevance drives engagement. One size fits none.

---

### 6. **Ignoring Mobile**
**Wrong:**
> Long paragraphs, tiny buttons, multiple columns

**Right:**
> - Short paragraphs (1-3 sentences)
> - Single column layout
> - Thumb-friendly CTA buttons (44px+ height)
> - Subject lines < 40 characters

**Why:** 60%+ emails opened on mobile. Design for thumbs.

---

### 7. **No Unsubscribe Option**
**Wrong:**
> Hidden or no unsubscribe link

**Right:**
> Clear, one-click unsubscribe in footer

**Why:** Legal requirement (CAN-SPAM, GDPR). Also respects users.

---

### 8. **Sending From "No-Reply"**
**Wrong:**
> From: noreply@company.com

**Right:**
> From: mira@settley.co or "Mira from Settley"

**Why:** "No-reply" says "we don't want to hear from you." Human names build trust.

---

### 9. **No Follow-Up on Clicks**
**Wrong:**
> User clicks CTA → Nothing happens → No follow-up

**Right:**
> User clicks CTA → Tracked → Follow-up email references their interest

**Why:** Click = intent. Follow up while warm.

---

### 10. **Generic Personalization**
**Wrong:**
> "Hi [FIRST_NAME], check out our amazing product!"

**Right:**
> "Hi Temisan, saw you're interested in UK property investment. Here's a guide specific to Manchester yields..."

**Why:** [FIRST_NAME] is lazy. Behavioral personalization = relevance.

## 📊 Email Performance Benchmarks

| Metric | Good | Great | Industry Average |
|--------|------|-------|------------------|
| **Open Rate** | 25%+ | 35%+ | 20% |
| **Click Rate** | 3%+ | 5%+ | 2% |
| **Unsubscribe** | <0.5% | <0.2% | 0.5% |
| **Conversion** | 2%+ | 5%+ | 1% |

**If below benchmarks:** Test subject lines, send times, segmentation.

## 🧪 Pre-Send Checklist

Before sending any sequence:

- [ ] **ONE primary CTA** per email
- [ ] **Mobile-optimized** (test on phone)
- [ ] **Subject line < 60 chars** (ideally < 40)
- [ ] **Preview text** extends subject (not repeats)
- [ ] **Unsubscribe link** visible in footer
- [ ] **From name** is human (not "noreply")
- [ ] **Links tracked** (UTM parameters)
- [ ] **Spam score** < 3/10 (use spam tester)
- [ ] **Plain text version** included
- [ ] **Send time** optimized for audience (B2B: weekdays, B2C: test weekends)

## 🔁 Common Revision Patterns

### Pattern 1: "Low Open Rates"
**Diagnosis:** Subject lines not compelling  
**Fix:** 
- Add specificity (numbers, outcomes)
- Create curiosity gap
- Test questions vs. statements
- A/B test emoji (polarizing but can boost opens)

---

### Pattern 2: "High Opens, Low Clicks"
**Diagnosis:** Email content doesn't deliver on subject promise  
**Fix:**
- Align content with subject line
- Make CTA more prominent
- Reduce friction (fewer links, clearer path)
- Add social proof near CTA

---

### Pattern 3: "High Unsubscribes"
**Diagnosis:** Sending too frequently or irrelevant content  
**Fix:**
- Reduce frequency (space out emails)
- Segment for relevance
- Add preference center (let users choose frequency)
- Survey unsubscribers (learn why)

---

### Pattern 4: "No Conversions"
**Diagnosis:** Asking too soon or unclear value prop  
**Fix:**
- Add more value emails before asking
- Clarify offer/benefit
- Add urgency or scarcity (if genuine)
- Include social proof near conversion point

## 📧 Subject Line Patterns That Work

### ✅ High Performers:
- **Numbers:** "3 ways to diversify into property"
- **How-to:** "How to earn rental income from £100"
- **Questions:** "Still looking for passive income?"
- **Direct:** "Your Settley early access is ready"
- **Story tease:** "The mistake I made with my first investment"
- **Urgency (real):** "24 hours left: Founder's Pass pricing"

### ❌ Low Performers:
- **Vague:** "Update from our team"
- **Salesy:** "BUY NOW!!! LIMITED OFFER!!!"
- **Clickbait:** "You won't BELIEVE what happened"
- **Generic:** "Newsletter #47"
- **All caps:** "IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT"

## ⏰ Timing Best Practices

| Sequence Type | Email 1 | Email 2 | Email 3 | Email 4+ |
|---------------|---------|---------|---------|----------|
| **Welcome** | Immediate | Day 1 | Day 3 | Weekly |
| **Onboarding** | Immediate | Day 2 | Day 4 | Day 7, 14, 21 |
| **Lead Nurture** | Day 0 | Day 3 | Day 7 | Bi-weekly |
| **Re-engagement** | Day 0 | Day 3 | Day 7 | Day 14 (final) |
| **Post-Purchase** | Immediate | Day 3 | Day 14 | Monthly |

**Test:** B2B (avoid weekends), B2C (test weekends), Time zones (send at local time)

## 🛡️ Compliance Checklist

- [ ] **Physical address** in footer (CAN-SPAM requirement)
- [ ] **Unsubscribe link** working (one-click preferred)
- [ ] **Privacy policy** linked
- [ ] **GDPR consent** recorded (EU subscribers)
- [ ] **No purchased lists** (only opted-in subscribers)
- [ ] **Honest subject lines** (no clickbait)
- [ ] **Clear identification** (who is sending)
