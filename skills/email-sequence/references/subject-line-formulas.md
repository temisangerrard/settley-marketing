# Subject Line Formulas That Get Opens

**Source:** Proven formulas from email marketing  
**Use:** When writing subject lines for any email type

---

## 🎯 Core Formulas

### 1. **The Question**
> "Still struggling with [pain point]?"

**Template:** `[Question about their situation]?`

**Examples:**
- "Still looking for passive income?"
- "Tired of estate agent fees?"
- "Want rental income without landlord headaches?"

**When:** Re-engagement, nurture sequences

---

### 2. **The How-To**
> "How to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe]"

**Template:** `How to [benefit] in [time]`

**Examples:**
- "How to earn rental income from £100"
- "How to diversify into property in 10 minutes"
- "How to build passive income in 2026"

**When:** Educational sequences, welcome emails

---

### 3. **The Number**
> "3 ways to [benefit]"

**Template:** `[Number] ways/tips/mistakes [topic]`

**Examples:**
- "3 ways to diversify into property"
- "5 mistakes first-time investors make"
- "7 passive income streams for 2026"

**When:** Educational content, nurture sequences

---

### 4. **The Direct**
> "[First name], your [thing] is ready"

**Template:** `[Name], [specific thing]`

**Examples:**
- "Temisan, your early access is ready"
- "Your Founder's Pass confirmation"
- "Your property investment guide inside"

**When:** Transactional, welcome sequences

---

### 5. **The Story Tease**
> "The mistake I made with [topic]"

**Template:** `The [adjective] [story element]`

**Examples:**
- "The mistake I made with my first investment"
- "Why I almost quit property investing"
- "The conversation that changed everything"

**When:** Story-driven emails, nurture sequences

---

### 6. **The Urgency (Real)**
> "24 hours left: [offer]"

**Template:** `[Time] left: [specific offer]`

**Examples:**
- "24 hours left: Founder's Pass pricing"
- "Last chance: Early bird pricing ends tonight"
- "Final reminder: Registration closes at midnight"

**When:** Time-sensitive offers (use sparingly, only when genuine)

---

### 7. **The Curiosity Gap**
> "The [unexpected thing] about [topic]"

**Template:** `The [surprising element] about [topic]`

**Examples:**
- "The surprising truth about UK property yields"
- "What 90% of investors get wrong about diversification"
- "The one thing holding you back from passive income"

**When:** Educational content, re-engagement

---

### 8. **The Benefit-First**
> "Earn [outcome] without [pain]"

**Template:** `[Benefit] without [objection]`

**Examples:**
- "Earn rental income without becoming a landlord"
- "Own property without £50k deposits"
- "Passive income without managing tenants"

**When:** Welcome sequences, conversion emails

---

### 9. **The Social Proof**
> "How [number] investors [achieved outcome]"

**Template:** `How [social proof] [result]`

**Examples:**
- "How 150+ investors earn passive income"
- "Why 67% of users upgrade to Founder's Pass"
- "What our top investors do differently"

**When:** Nurture sequences, conversion emails

---

### 10. **The Personalization**
> "[Name], I noticed [specific behavior]"

**Template:** `[Name], [specific observation]`

**Examples:**
- "Temisan, I noticed you're interested in Manchester properties"
- "Saw you downloaded the guide — here's the next step"
- "Your portfolio is 80% complete"

**When:** Behavioral triggers, onboarding sequences

## 🧪 Subject Line Testing Framework

**Test each subject line on:**

1. **Clarity** — Can they understand it in < 2 seconds?
2. **Relevance** — Does it match their interests/situation?
3. **Curiosity** — Does it make them want to know more?
4. **Specificity** — Are there numbers, names, or concrete details?
5. **Length** — Is it < 60 characters (ideally < 40)?

**Score:** 1-5 on each. Total ≥20 = strong subject line.

## 🚫 Common Mistakes

### ❌ Too Vague
> "Update from our team"

**Fix:** Add specificity
> "Your early access to Settley is ready"

---

### ❌ Too Long
> "We're Excited to Announce Our New Feature That Will Help You Invest in Property More Easily Than Ever Before"

**Fix:** Cut 70%
> "New Feature: Invest in Property in 3 Clicks"

---

### ❌ Clickbait
> "You Won't BELIEVE What Happened Next!!!"

**Fix:** Be honest
> "The mistake I made with my first investment"

---

### ❌ All Caps
> "IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT INSIDE"

**Fix:** Use normal case
> "Important: Your account verification"

---

### ❌ Spammy
> "BUY NOW!!! LIMITED OFFER!!! ACT FAST!!!"

**Fix:** Be helpful
> "Founder's Pass: 48 hours left at early bird pricing"
