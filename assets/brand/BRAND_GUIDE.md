# Settley Brand Guide

## Voice & Tone
- **Professional but approachable**
- **Educational without being condescending**
- **Direct and clear**
- **Trustworthy and transparent**

## Key Messages
- Democratizing real estate investment through tokenization
- Making property investment accessible and liquid
- Transparent, secure, and compliant
- Built for both new and experienced investors

## Technical Terms
- **Tokenization** - Always explain in simple terms first
- **Blockchain** - Focus on benefits, not technology
- **Real Estate Investment** - Emphasize accessibility
- **Liquidity** - Explain practical implications

## Content Principles
1. **Educate First** - Every piece should teach something valuable
2. **Show, Don't Tell** - Use real examples and case studies
3. **Address Concerns** - Proactively tackle common objections
4. **Be Specific** - Use numbers and concrete details
5. **Focus on Benefits** - Lead with user value

## Writing Guidelines
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences concise
- Break up long paragraphs
- Use headers for scanning
- Include clear calls to action

_Note: This is a living document - will evolve based on what resonates with our audience._
