## Lessons Learned

### From Nano Banana 2 Analysis
The competitive analysis Temisan shared today confirms:
- **Speed matters** - Clones appear within 24 hours
- **Long-tail keywords are vulnerable** - "free online" and "pricing" terms get captured
- **AI Overview can backfire** - Google's AI cited competitor pricing over official

Our cron-based "set it and forget it" approach was fundamentally wrong. We need:
- Faster iteration (manual execution allows mid-task course correction)
- Human judgment (knowing when to pivot vs. push through)
- Verifiable velocity (daily commits > weekly cron bursts)

### From Cron Failure
- **System building ≠ growth** - Built 12 cron jobs, 75% failed, 0 growth resulted
- **Execution > automation** - Manual execution with proof beats broken automation
- **Measure outcomes, not output** - 5 emails sent with proof > 100 cron runs that timeout
