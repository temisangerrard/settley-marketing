## What Settley Does

Property tokenization platform enabling fractional real estate ownership.

**Mechanism:**
1. Property is placed in SPV (Special Purpose Vehicle) or Delaware LLC
2. SPV ownership is tokenized into ERC-20 tokens on Base (Ethereum L2)
3. Tokens are sold to investors (fractional ownership)
4. Rental income is distributed via smart contracts
5. Tokens can be traded on secondary market (liquidity)

**Key Differentiator:** Infrastructure approach, not marketplace. "The AWS of tokenized real estate."
