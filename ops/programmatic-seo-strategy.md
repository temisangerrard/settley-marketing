### Option 2: Custom AI Pipeline (Recommended)
**What it does:** Same as Byword, but custom-built for Settley
**Tech Stack:**
- **LLM:** Claude 3.5 Sonnet (via OpenRouter)
- **Data:** UK property data (Rightmove, Zoopla APIs)
- **Templates:** Custom (Settley brand voice)
- **Hosting:** settley.co/blog/[slug]

**Cost:**
- API calls: ~$0.01-0.03 per article
- 1,000 articles = ~$10-30
- One-time dev: 10-20 hours

**Pros:**
- Cheaper long-term
- UK-specific content
- Settley brand voice
- Full control

**Cons:**
- Dev time required
- Maintenance needed

**Approach:**
1. Use Byword for initial 500-1,000 pages (fast launch)

3. Human review for all content (quality control)

## ⚖️ Ethical Guidelines (Our Approach)

**What We WILL Do:**
✅ Original content (not copied from competitors)
✅ Human review (AI-generated, human-edited)
✅ User value (helpful guides, not thin content)
✅ Transparency (disclose AI use if asked)
✅ UK-specific (local data, UK regulations)

**What We WON'T Do:**
❌ Copy competitor content (Jake Ward's "heist" tactic)
❌ Thin content (AI spam with no value)
❌ Misleading claims (exaggerated returns, false promises)
❌ Keyword stuffing (unnatural keyword density)
❌ Hidden text/links (black-hat SEO tactics)
