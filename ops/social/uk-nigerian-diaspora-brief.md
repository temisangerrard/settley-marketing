## Platform-Specific Tactics

### Twitter/X
- **Frequency:** 2-3 tweets/day
- **Format:** Threads (5-10 tweets) + single tweets with visuals
- **Hashtags:** #UKProperty #NigeriansInUK #PropertyInvestment #PassiveIncome
- **Engagement:** Reply to UK property tweets, Nigerian finance accounts

### LinkedIn
- **Frequency:** 3 posts/week + daily comments
- **Format:** Long-form posts (300-500 words) + articles (800+ words)
- **Engagement:** Comment on UK property investor posts
- **Networking:** Connect with Nigerian professionals in UK (finance, tech, healthcare)

- **Frequency:** 1-2 messages/week (NOT broadcast)
- **Format:** Personal messages to group admins
- **Ask:** "Can you share this with your group?"
- **Incentive:** Referrer gets founder pricing

- **Groups:** Nigerians in UK, Nigerian Professionals UK, UK Property Investors
- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts/week (varied content)
- **Format:** Educational first, CTA second
- **Engagement:** Answer questions, don't just promote

## Tone Guidelines

**Do:**
- ✅ Educational first, promotional second

- ✅ Social proof (150 users, FCA-regulated)
- ✅ Urgency (founder pricing ends at launch)

**Don't:**
- ❌ Hype ("Get rich quick!")
- ❌ Guarantees ("Guaranteed 10% returns!")
- ❌ Pressure ("Sign up NOW or miss out!")
- ❌ Vague claims ("Best investment ever!")
