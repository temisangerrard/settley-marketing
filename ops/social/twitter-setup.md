## 📝 Cross-Posting Strategy

### Article → Thread Conversion
Each Medium article becomes:
- **1 main thread** (key insights + link to article)
- **2-3 supporting tweets** (quotes, stats, questions)
- **Engagement tweets** (reply to comments, share reactions)

### Template: Article Thread

**Main Thread (5 tweets):**
1. Hook + Value Prop
2. Key Stat/Insight  
3. Problem/Solution
4. Call to Action
5. Link + Hashtags

**Supporting Tweets:**
- Quote most impactful paragraph
- Share "did you know?" stat
- Ask community question
- Share personal connection to topic
