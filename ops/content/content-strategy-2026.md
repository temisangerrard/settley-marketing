## North Star

**Build trust through education, not hype.** Every piece of content should either:
1. Teach something genuine about tokenized real estate or onchain gaming
2. Show real progress on what we're building
3. Challenge a misconception in the market
4. Invite collaboration from the right people (investors, partners, users)

## Voice Guidelines

**Do:**
- Sound like a smart friend explaining something they understand
- Use specific numbers and examples
- Admit what we don't know
- Challenge ideas respectfully
- Show, don't tell (screenshots > claims)

**Don't:**
- Use marketing jargon ("revolutionary," "game-changing")
- Overpromise ("coming soon" without dates)
- Hedge every statement ("we think," "maybe," "potentially")
- Sound like a corporate account
- Engage with trolls or bad-faith critics

## Risk Management

**What could go wrong:**
1. **Overposting:** Burn out audience → Stick to cadence
2. **Underposting:** Lose momentum → Minimum 3 tweets/week
3. **Controversy:** Hot take backfires → Apologize if wrong, clarify if misunderstood
4. **Silence:** No engagement after 2 weeks → Pivot content type or topic

**Escalation:**
- If a post gets significant negative attention: Pause, assess, Temisan decision on response
- If partnership discussions start: Move to DM, then formal intro email
- If press inquiry arrives: Respond within 24 hours, loop in Temisan for product claims
