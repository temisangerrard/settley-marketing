## 📋 Key FCA Findings

### Timeline
| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| **Dec 2025** | Cryptoasset Regulations published |
| **Feb 2026** | CP25/28 consultation closed (fund tokenisation) |
| **Sept 2026** | FCA "cryptoasset gateway" opens (applications) |
| **Oct 2027** | Full regulatory regime in force |

### What's Regulated
**Qualifying Cryptoassets:**
- Tokens representing ownership (like Settley property tokens)
- Security tokens (equity in SPV = regulated)
- Stablecoins (if we use USDC for distributions)

**Regulated Activities:**
- Issuing tokens (public offer = restricted)
- Dealing/arranging transactions (intermediary activity)
- Operating trading platform (secondary market)

### Public Offers = Restricted
**Key Rule:** Public offers of qualifying cryptoassets are **prohibited unless exemption applies**.

**Exemptions:**
1. **Private placement** — To professional/institutional clients only
2. **Existing shareholders** — Rights issues to existing holders
3. **Small offers** — Under €8M total (but still needs disclosure)
4. **High net worth individuals** — Certified HNWI, sophisticated investor

**Retail Participation:**
- ✅ Allowed with enhanced safeguards
- ✅ Clear risk disclosures required
- ✅ Express client consent needed
- ❌ No misleading promises
- ❌ No guaranteed returns
