## ⚠️ Clone/Clone War Protocol

Based on Nano Banana 2 analysis, expect clones within 24 hours of any major launch.

### Early Warning Signs
- Domain registrations matching our brand/keywords
- New sites ranking for our target keywords
- Social media accounts mimicking our branding
- API resellers offering our services at lower prices

### Response Protocol
1. **Day 1-3:** Document and monitor (don't overreact to single clones)
2. **Day 4-7:** Assess threat level (traffic, funding, team quality)
3. **Day 8-14:** Counter-move if threat is significant (content, SEO, partnerships)
4. **Day 15+:** Consider legal action if brand infringement is clear

### Defensive Moats
- **Speed:** Launch features faster than clones can copy
- **Community:** Build investor network effects
- **Regulatory:** Leverage FCA compliance as differentiator
- **Content:** Establish thought leadership (harder to copy)
- **Partnerships:** Exclusive developer/institutional relationships
