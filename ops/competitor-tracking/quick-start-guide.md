### 2. Manual SERP Check Spreadsheet (10 minutes)

Open: `ops/competitor-tracking/serp-baseline-2026-03-20.csv`

**Daily Task (5 min each morning):**
1. Google each keyword (use incognito mode)
2. Note Settley's position (if ranking)
3. Note Lofty.ai position
4. Note any new domains in top 10
5. Screenshot if significant changes

**Pro Tips:**
- Use `&gl=uk` or `&gl=us` in Google URL to force location
- Use `&num=10` to show 10 results
- Example: `https://google.com/search?q=tokenized+real+estate&gl=uk&num=10`

## 🚨 Alert Triggers (When to Notify Temisan Immediately)

**RED ALERT (Message within 1 hour):**
- New well-funded competitor launches (announces funding)
- Lofty.ai drops prices significantly (>20%)
- Major platform announces tokenized real estate feature
- Settley brand keyword drops below #5

**YELLOW ALERT (Message within 24 hours):**
- New competitor in top 10 for main keywords
- Competitor launches major feature
- Negative sentiment spike on social media
- AI Overview starts citing competitor over us

**GREEN (Log in daily report, no immediate action):**
- Minor SERP position changes (±2 positions)
- Routine competitor blog posts
- Normal social media mentions
