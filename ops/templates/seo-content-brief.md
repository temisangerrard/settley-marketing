## 1. Keyword Research Framework

### Primary Keyword Selection
- **Search volume:** 100-1000/month (achievable for new domain)
- **Intent:** Informational or commercial investigation
- **Competition:** Low-medium (avoid head terms like "real estate investing")
- **UK/US specificity:** Include geography in keyword

### UK Keyword Examples
- "tokenized real estate UK"
- "fractional property investment UK"
- "property investment under £1000"
- "how to invest in property with little money UK"
- "real estate crowdfunding UK"

### US Keyword Examples

- "fractional real estate investing"

- "real estate crowdfunding for beginners"

### Tools

## 2. Article Structure Template

### Meta Elements
```
Title: [Primary Keyword] + [Benefit/Year] (55-60 chars)
Meta Description: [Problem] + [Solution] + [CTA] (150-160 chars)
URL Slug: /blog/[primary-keyword-hyphenated]
```

### H1
- Include primary keyword
- Clear benefit or promise
- Example: "Tokenized Real Estate UK: Complete Guide (2026)"

### Introduction (150-200 words)
- Hook: Surprising stat or relatable problem
- Context: Why this matters now
- Promise: What reader will learn
- **No Settley mention yet**

### H2: The Problem (200-300 words)
- Traditional property investment barriers
- Specific UK/US context (deposit requirements, mortgage rules)
- Data point: % of adults locked out

### H2: What Is [Solution]? (300-400 words)
- Clear definition
- How it works (simplified)
- Analogy for clarity (e.g., "Like a REIT, but modernized")

### H2: How [Solution] Works (400-500 words)
- Step-by-step breakdown
- Include diagram/visual description
- Address common concerns (legitimacy, regulation)

### H2: Benefits (200-300 words)
- 3-5 specific benefits with data
- Compare to traditional approach
- **Settley mention OK here if natural**

### H2: Risks & Considerations (200-300 words)
- Honest discussion of risks
- Regulatory context (FCA for UK, SEC for US)
- Builds trust through transparency

### H2: Getting Started (150-200 words)
- Actionable first steps
- Minimum investment amounts
- **Settley mention OK here**

### Conclusion (100-150 words)
- Summarize key points
- Final thought or call-to-action
- **Soft Settley mention if fits**

## 3. Content Guidelines

### Tone
- Educational (80%) > promotional (20%)
- Direct, clear, no jargon without explanation
- Confident but not arrogant
- UK/US spelling matches target audience

### Length
- Minimum: 1500 words
- Target: 2000 words
- Maximum: 3000 words (diminishing returns)

### Data Requirements
- Minimum 3 specific data points
- Sources must be credible (gov, established research)
- UK data for UK articles (£, UK cities, UK regulations)
- US data for US articles ($, US cities, US regulations)

### Settley Integration Rules
- **Mention max 2-3 times** in 2000 words
- Only where it adds value (not forced)
- Focus on differentiators: UK compliance, £500 entry, curated deals
- Never make promises about returns

## 5. Competitor Gap Analysis Checklist

Before writing, answer:

1. **Who ranks top 5 for target keyword?**
   - List URLs
   - Note their angle/approach

2. **What do they cover well?**
   - Don't duplicate — link out instead

3. **What do they miss?**
   - UK-specific context?
   - Regulatory clarity?
   - Honest risk discussion?
   - Specific data points?

4. **What's our unique angle?**
   - Compliance-first positioning
   - Lower minimum entry
   - Curated vs. open marketplace
   - UK market focus (vs. US-only competitors)

5. **What internal links can we add?**
   - Link to related Settley content
   - Link to Founder's Pass if relevant
