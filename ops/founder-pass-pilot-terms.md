**Example:**
- Invest £2,500 → 5,000 tokens (5% equity)
- Invest £5,000 → 10,000 tokens (10% equity)
- Invest £10,000 → 20,000 tokens (20% equity)

### 4. Dividend Rights

**Distribution Policy:**
- 80% of net rental income distributed quarterly to token holders
- 20% retained in SPV for maintenance reserve + contingencies

**Distribution Method:**
- GBP bank transfer (to verified investor accounts)
- OR stablecoin (USDC/USDT) on investor request
- Quarterly distributions (March, June, September, December)

**Projected Yield:**
- Gross rental yield: 6-8% (property-dependent)
- Net yield (after costs): 4.5-6.5%
- Investor share (70% of SPV): 3.2-4.6% annual return on investment

**Example (£5,000 investment):**
- Property value: £135,000
- Annual rent: £8,400 (£700/month)
- Net income (after costs): £6,000
- Investor share (70% of SPE): £4,200 total
- Your share (£5k of £35k = 14.3% of investor pool): £600/year
- **Your yield:** 12% on £5,000 investment

### 5. Capital Appreciation

**Property Sale:**
- Requires majority vote (>50% of tokens)
- Sale proceeds distributed pro-rata after:
  - Mortgage payoff
  - Transaction costs (agent fees, legal)
  - Capital gains tax (if applicable)

**Appreciation Split:**
- Investors: 70% of net proceeds
- Settley: 30% of net proceeds

**Example (5-year hold, 20% appreciation):**
- Purchase: £135,000
- Sale: £162,000
- Net proceeds (after costs): £150,000
- Investor pool (70%): £105,000
- Your share (£5k investment = 14.3% of pool): £15,000
- **Your gain:** £10,000 on £5,000 investment (200% return, 14.9% IRR)

### 6. Secondary Market (Token Transfers)

**Phase 1 (Months 0-12):** Hold-to-rent
- No secondary market
- Tokens are illiquid
- Focus on dividend income

**Phase 2 (Month 13+):** Whitelist transfers
- Tokens transferable to verified eligible investors
- Settley maintains whitelist (KYC/AML required)
- Transfer fee: 2% (paid by seller, goes to SPV)
- Price: Negotiated between buyer/seller (no Settley involvement)

**Phase 3 (Month 24+):** Buyback option (discretionary)
- Settley may offer to buy back tokens at fair market value
- Not guaranteed — at Settley's discretion
- Fair market value = pro-rata SPV net asset value

### 7. Investor Rights

**Voting Rights:**
- 1 token = 1 vote
- Major decisions require >50% vote:
  - Property sale
  - Refinancing
  - Major capital expenditure (>£5,000)
  - Change of property manager

**Information Rights:**
- Quarterly financial statements (rental income, expenses, distributions)
- Annual property valuation
- Ad-hoc updates on material events (tenant changes, major repairs)

**Pre-emption Rights:**
- First right of refusal on future Settley tokenized properties
- Pro-rata allocation in future rounds (if desired)

## Risk Factors

### ⚠️ High-Risk Investment — Read Carefully

**1. Capital Loss Risk**
- Property values can decrease
- You may lose some or all of your investment
- Only invest what you can afford to lose

**2. Illiquidity Risk**
- No guaranteed secondary market for 12+ months
- You may not be able to exit when you want
- This is a long-term investment (5-10 years recommended)

**3. Rental Income Risk**
- Property may be vacant (void periods)
- Tenant may not pay (arrears, eviction costs)
- Maintenance costs may exceed reserves

**4. Interest Rate Risk**
- Mortgage is variable rate (currently ~5-6%)
- Rate increases reduce net income
- May affect property value

**5. Regulatory Risk**
- UK property/tokenization regulations may change
- Tax treatment may change
- FCA may introduce new requirements

**6. Technology Risk**
- Smart contract bugs (mitigated: audits, testnet first)
- Blockchain network issues
- Wallet security (investor responsibility)

**7. Settley Execution Risk**
- We're early-stage (no track record)
- Property acquisition may take longer than expected
- Tokenization may face technical delays

## Tax Treatment (UK Investors)

### Rental Income
- Taxed as property income (not dividend income)
- Reported on Self Assessment tax return
- SPV provides annual income statement
- **Rate:** 20% (basic), 40% (higher), 45% (additional)

### Capital Gains
- Gain on property sale = capital gain
- Annual exempt amount: £3,000 (2026/27)
- **Rate:** 18% (basic), 24% (higher/additional) for residential property

### Stamp Duty
- Paid by SPV on property purchase (3% surcharge)
- Already factored into investment terms
- No additional SDLT for investors

### VAT
- Residential rent is VAT-exempt
- No VAT on rental income or distributions

**⚠️ Not Tax Advice:** Consult a qualified tax advisor for your situation.
