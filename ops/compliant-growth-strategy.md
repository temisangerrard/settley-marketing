# Settley Growth Strategy — FCA-Compliant SEO & Content

**Date:** 2026-03-11
**Status:** Active (compliant with FCA CP25/28, CP25/40, CP25/41)

---

## 🎯 Strategy Summary

**Goal:** Grow Settley audience + waitlist WITHOUT violating FCA rules on public cryptoasset offers.

**Approach:**
1. **Educational content first** — No investment language
2. **Waitlist = email capture** — Not investment commitment
3. **Private outreach only** — Founder's Pass members (existing relationship)
4. **Public = informational** — No "invest now" claims

## ⚖️ Compliance Checklist (Before Publishing)

### Every Article Must Have:
- [ ] "Not financial advice" disclaimer
- [ ] "Not an investment offer" statement
- [ ] Risk warnings (if discussing investments)
- [ ] Authoritative sources (FCA, ONS, Land Registry)
- [ ] No return promises
- [ ] No "invest now" language
- [ ] UK residents only noted (if applicable)

### Every Social Post Must Have:
- [ ] No specific return claims
- [ ] No urgency ("act now", "limited spots")
- [ ] Educational tone (not salesy)
- [ ] Links to full articles (not direct to waitlist only)

## 🎯 Growth Without Violations

**What Works:**
✅ Educational content (ranks on Google, builds trust)
✅ Data-driven articles (authoritative, shareable)
✅ Regulation explainers (positions Settley as expert)
✅ Waitlist (email capture, no commitment)
✅ Private outreach (Founder's Pass, existing relationship)

**What Doesn't Work:**
❌ Public investment solicitation (FCA violation)
❌ Return promises (misleading, potential fraud)
❌ Urgency tactics ("limited spots", "act now")
❌ Guarantees ("risk-free", "guaranteed returns")
