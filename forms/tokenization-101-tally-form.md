## Form Structure

### Form Title: Tokenization 101 - Free Guide

### Form Description:
Get instant access to our comprehensive guide on tokenized real estate investing. Learn how to start with just £50 and build a diversified property portfolio.

### Form Fields:

#### 1. First Name
- Type: Short text
- Required: Yes
- Description: Your first name

#### 2. Email Address  
- Type: Email
- Required: Yes
- Description: We'll send the guide to this email

#### 3. Investment Experience
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Options:
  - Complete beginner (never invested in property)
  - Some experience (owned property or invested in REITs)
  - Experienced investor (multiple properties, familiar with tokenization)

#### 4. Investment Timeline
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Options:
  - Looking to invest immediately (within 30 days)
  - Researching for future investment (3-6 months)
  - Just curious about the technology (no immediate plans)

#### 5. Preferred Property Type
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Options:
  - Residential (apartments, houses)
  - Commercial (office, retail)
  - Mixed-use properties
  - No preference

#### 6. Investment Amount Range
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Options:
  - £500-£1,000
  - £1,000-£5,000
  - £5,000-£10,000
  - £10,000+

#### 7. Questions or Comments
- Type: Long text
- Required: No
- Description: Any specific questions about tokenized real estate investing?
