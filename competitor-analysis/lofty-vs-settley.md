# Competitor Analysis: Lofty vs Settley

## Investment Options Comparison

### Settley
- **Entry Point**: From £50 (Founder's Pass)
- **Property Types**: UK residential focus (London, Manchester)
- **Target**: UK/US retail investors
- **Approach**: Education-first, transparent tokenization

### Lofty
- **Entry Point**: $10,000+ minimums
- **Property Types**: US residential multi-family
- **Target**: US accredited investors
- **Approach**: DeFi yield farming partnerships (12-15% APR)

---

## Fee Structure Comparison

### Settley
- **Founder's Pass**: £97 one-time
- **Platform Fees**: 1-2% on transactions
- **No hidden costs**: Transparent fee structure
- **Currency**: GBP focus for UK market

### Lofty
- **Management Fees**: 1% annually
- **Transaction Fees**: 2-5% per trade
- **Yield Farming**: Additional DeFi protocol fees
- **Currency**: USD focus for US market

---

## Transparency & Access

### Settley
- **Property Details**: Full disclosure of property specs, rental history, tenant information
- **Legal Structure**: Clear token-to-property ownership mapping
- **Risk Disclosure**: Transparent about market risks, liquidity constraints
- **Regulatory Compliance**: UK-focused compliance framework

### Lofty
- **Property Details**: Limited public information on individual properties
- **Legal Structure**: Complex DeFi protocol layers
- **Risk Disclosure**: Emphasis on high APY returns, less on property fundamentals
- **Regulatory Compliance**: US SEC compliance focus

---

## Risk & Regulation Section

### Settley Risk Management
- **Diversification**: Multiple properties, UK geographic spread
- **Liquidity**: Secondary market for token trading
- **Market Risk**: Focus on established UK rental markets
- **Regulatory**: FCA-compliant structure, UK investor protection

### Lofty Risk Management
- **Concentration**: US market focus, multi-family exposure
- **Liquidity**: DeFi protocol dependencies
- **Market Risk**: Higher yield targets increase risk profile
- **Regulatory**: SEC compliance, accredited investor requirements

### Regulatory Differences
- **UK Approach**: FCA oversight, retail investor access, consumer protection focus
- **US Approach**: SEC oversight, accredited investor gates, DeFi regulation uncertainty

- **Lower Entry**: £50 vs $10,000+ competitors
