# Growth System

## Tracking & Measurement
- Weekly metrics review
- Monthly growth reports
- Experiment tracking
- Conversion funnel analysis

## Key Metrics
1. **Acquisition**
   - Website visitors (UK/US focus)
   - Traffic sources
   - SEO rankings for key terms
   
2. **Activation**
   - Signup rate
   - Market distribution of users
   - First-time property view
   
3. **Revenue**
   - Founder's Pass conversions
   - Investment volume
   - Revenue per user

4. **Retention**
   - Monthly active users
   - Repeat investors
   - User satisfaction scores

## Experiment Framework
Each growth experiment should document:
- Hypothesis
- Success metrics
- Implementation plan
- Results & learnings
- Next steps

## Priority Areas
1. UK/US Market Entry
2. SEO & Content Strategy
3. User Acquisition Channels
4. Conversion Optimization
5. Community Building

## Weekly Routine
- Monday: Metrics review
- Tuesday: Content planning
- Wednesday: Experiment review
- Thursday: Content creation
- Friday: Analytics & optimization

_This system will evolve based on what we learn works best for Settley._
