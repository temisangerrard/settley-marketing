Everything you need to know about tokenized real estate investment

Tokenized real estate is the process of converting ownership rights in a property into digital tokens on a blockchain. Each token represents a share of the property, allowing multiple investors to own fractional portions. This makes real estate investing accessible to everyone, not just those who can afford to buy entire properties.

Traditional property investment requires large deposits (£20,000+), long-term commitments (6+ month sale cycles), and management responsibilities. Settley allows you to start from £50, provides liquidity through secondary markets, and handles all property management. You simply receive your share of rental income and property appreciation.

Settley operates under FCA regulations and uses legal structures that ensure your tokens are backed by real property ownership. Unlike speculative crypto assets, tokenized real estate represents actual bricks and mortar. However, as with all investments, there are risks including market fluctuations and property-specific risks that are clearly disclosed.

Unlike traditional property which can take 6+ months to sell, Settley tokens can be sold on our secondary market. This provides much greater liquidity - you can exit your investment position in days rather than years. The process is similar to selling shares in a company rather than selling a physical building.

Like all property investments, tokenized real estate is subject to market conditions. However, you benefit from diversification across multiple properties and locations, which reduces risk. You also continue receiving rental income throughout market downturns. Settley focuses on properties in resilient markets with strong rental demand to minimize volatility.
