# Lofty.ai Competitive Deep Dive
**Research Date:** 2026-02-20
**Purpose:** Identify positioning gaps for Settley's UK unlock strategy

## Key Findings

### Geography Gap (Our Biggest Opportunity)
- **Lofty is US-only**: Every property is US-based, US investors only
- **No international expansion**: No mention of UK/EU markets anywhere
- **Regulatory focus**: Only has US accreditations
- **Settley angle**: "Real estate tokenization for the UK market"

### Investment Mechanics Differences
**Lofty's approach:**
- Minimum: $50-$90 per property
- Algorand blockchain (not Ethereum like us)
- Daily rental distributions (we do monthly)
- They handle property management (we keep owner control)
- 2.5% fee structure (similar to our 2-3%)

### Property Types & Focus
**Current:**
- 240+ properties (higher than we thought)
- Price range: $80-$200 per token
- Focus on single-family homes, mostly B/C class neighborhoods
- Locations: Atlanta, Tampa, Chicago, Charlotte
- Target: Rent yields 6-12%

### Educational Content Analysis
**High-performing topics:**
- "What is rental yield?"
- "How to build real estate wealth"
- "Fractional ownership explained"
- "Diversification strategies"

**Content gaps we can exploit:**
- No UK-specific tax advice
- Nothing about international investors
- No SPV/legal structure explanations
- Missing "what you actually own" clarity
