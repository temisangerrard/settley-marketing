# Initial Target Cities Analysis for Settley

## Selection Criteria
1. Property Investment Metrics
   - Rental yields
   - Capital appreciation potential
   - Average property prices
   - Transaction volume

2. Market Readiness
   - Tech-savvy population
   - Existing proptech adoption
   - Investment property demand
   - International investor interest

3. Strategic Value
   - Media presence
   - Financial hub status
   - Property technology ecosystem
   - Regulatory environment

## UK Recommended Cities

### 1. Manchester
**Why Manchester:**
- Highest rental yields in UK (up to 8%)
- Major regeneration projects
- Large student population (investment opportunity)
- Growing tech hub
- More affordable entry point than London
- Strong international investor interest

**Strategic Approach:**
- Focus on student housing tokenization
- Partner with local property developers
- Target young professional investors
- Highlight regeneration areas

### 2. London
**Why London:**
- Global financial center
- Established proptech ecosystem
- High international visibility
- Strong regulatory framework
- Premier league for property tech startups

**Strategic Approach:**
- Focus on prime locations for fractional ownership
- Target international investors
- Leverage fintech/proptech networks
- Position as flagship market

### 3. Birmingham
**Why Birmingham:**
- Second largest UK city
- HS2 rail link increasing appeal
- Strong price growth forecasts
- Large young professional population
- More affordable entry point

**Strategic Approach:**
- Focus on new developments
- Target first-time property investors
- Highlight infrastructure improvements
- Connect with local property networks

## US Recommended Cities

### 1. Austin
**Why Austin:**
- Tech hub with high-income young professionals
- Strong population growth
- Active real estate market
- Crypto/blockchain friendly
- Growing faster than traditional gateway cities

**Strategic Approach:**
- Target tech workers
- Focus on new developments
- Highlight tech ecosystem
- Partner with local crypto communities

### 2. Miami
**Why Miami:**
- Crypto-friendly administration
- High international appeal
- Strong rental market
- Active property technology scene
- Gateway to Latin American investors

**Strategic Approach:**
- Build relationships with crypto/real estate influencers
- Target international investors
- Focus on luxury condos for fractional ownership
- Leverage Miami Tech Week

### 3. Denver
**Why Denver:**
- Growing tech scene
- Strong millennial population
- Active real estate market
- High quality of life appeal
- More affordable than coastal cities

**Strategic Approach:**
- Target young professionals
- Focus on residential properties
- Highlight lifestyle benefits
- Connect with local tech communities
